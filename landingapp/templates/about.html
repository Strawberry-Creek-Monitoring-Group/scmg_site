Who We Are

The Strawberry Creek Monitoring Group was founded in 2024, as an off-branch of the Strawberry Creek Restoration Project, which began in 1987.

We work to understand the ecology of Strawberry Creek through the use of Open-Source hardware and software!

During 2024-2025, we will be funded by The Green Initiative Fund - afterwards, the Cal Environmental Initiative will support our mission of helping campuses learn and grow with their local watersheds.

In 2023, a significant Creek spill known as the Harvey Hall Diesel spill occurred, causing extensive damage. Much of this damage could have been prevented with proper detection systems.

Currently, there is no creek oil detection system in place at Strawberry Creek, and existing commercial options are expensive.

As a result, we are aiming to develop our own detection model, one that not only addresses these issues but can also be adapted and expanded to other schools facing similar challenges.
