Welcome to Strawberry Creek!

Strawberry Creek travels throughout the UC Berkeley Campus, and has been an integral part of our environment since the University's founding in 1868. Home to several endangered/ threatened species and native wildlife, Strawberry Creek is an urban watershed teeming with life. It also serves as an educational resource for several courses on campus.
